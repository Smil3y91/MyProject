Rabbit Reads Literary Agency LLC

Rabbit Reads is a literary agency offering representation for authors of fictional works. We wish to welcome in open arms both new and established writers and pride ourselves in treating both with the professionalism and dedication you deserve. At Rabbit Reads, we understand the plight of the author and truly wish to make the most of representing you, as your best interests and creativity are always at the heart of our endeavors.

Our representation at Rabbit Reads currently includes developmental editing services and aiding in the publication of your works. We offer written contracts for all accepted agent/author relationships and feel that this relationship is the most valuable and rewarding aspect of our career as an agency.Rabbit Reads strives as well to stay up to date on current writer and publisher markets as well as reader interests to ensure the most effective representation possible. After all, the only way for us to succeed is if our wonderful writers succeed.

If you are interested in achieving representation for your finished work, please go to our “Submissions” page to see how best to share your amazing hard work with us. At Rabbit Reads, we feel that no idea is a bad idea and we hope you feel comfortable sharing your skills, creativity, and passion with us. We understand the effort that goes into crafting a manuscript and should you choose to pursue a relationship with Rabbit Reads, we promise to handle your work with the same care, love, and respect that you created it with.

Submission

If you are looking to gain representation of your work through Rabbit Reads Literary Agency, please take a minute to familiarize yourself with the submission process below:

How to Submit Your Work:
  • Make sure that you have a finished work that is as ready for publishing as you feel you can achieve. We are happy to help with edits that improve the work but your novel should start as something to be proud of.
  • Send an email with “Novel Submission” as the subject to email@emai.com. Your email should include:
  • A short introduction to yourself in the body (your name, how you like to be referred to/addressed, and why you chose Rabbit Reads).
  • Three attachments containing your submission pieces:
  • A one to two page query letter.
  • A synopsis of your fiction novel (two pages maximum). We know it hurts, but please include all the plot twists.
  • The first three chapters of your novel.
  • Rabbit Reads tries to respond to everyone but due to submission volume, wait time may vary. Please refrain from emailing us about your work if you haven’t yet received a response; if you have been waiting at least three weeks since your submission please feel free to reach out and ensure we received your work.
Formatting Guidelines for Your Attachments:
  • Please be sure to have an appropriate font and size (12pt Times New Roman preferred).
  • Your work should be double spaced with zero additional space between paragraphs (you can find this in paragraph formatting). Indent using “Tab” and not the space bar.
  • Your work should be aligned left, not justified.
  • Include a title page with your novel’s title and your name.
  • For each page after the title page, include a header with the title, your last name, and the page number aligned right.
